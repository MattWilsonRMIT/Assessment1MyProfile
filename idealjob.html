WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPER
( DEVOPS / FRONT END )

It is my belief that web based applications are going to become increasingly relevant as we move more and more towards hosted services. Products like Google Stadia, Azure or your browser-based Netflix and Spotify are excellent examples of the direction I believe we are heading in. As a result, this position is not only an excellent career choice to commit to, but it is also an opportunity to be at the forefront of web-based apps in an environment and setting that aligns with my person values. I would be working alongside some of Australia's leading scientific minds in a field that I am extremely passionate about, web design and web-based application development.
The chance to positively impact the daily lives of Australian's while
problem solving and innovating is exactly what I am looking for in a career.

Skills required to succeed

I will need a high level of expertise in Angular, JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS along with previous experience working around automation and AWS.
The ability to troubleshoot problems that have potentially never been explored, work in a very tight, high performance team and maintain drive and a positive trajectory through every aspect of development.

Skills I currently have

As it is, I have spent some time with HTML5, CSS, SQL (sqlsrv/PDO), Javacript and PHP designing functional local web pages with an applied work based goal in mind. These "dashboards" were created to streamline work process for our team within the office.

What I will need to succeed

A continued effort in learning while expanding my knowledge base of the industry and relevant programming languages and how to apply them will hopefully provide me with the experience and ability to fulfill the requirements of this role and enable me to contribute and create in an environment where the goal is innovation.
