The Logistician

Personality ISTJ-A

"The Logistician personality type is thought to be the most abundant, making up around 13% of the population. Their defining characteristics of integrity, practical logic and tireless dedication to duty make Logisticians a vital core to many families, as well as organizations that uphold traditions, rules and standards, such as law offices, regulatory bodies and military.
People with the Logistician personality type enjoy taking responsibility for their actions, and take pride in the work they do ‚Äì when working towards a goal, Logisticians hold back none of their time and energy completing each relevant task with accuracy and patience."

There is much of the test results that resonate with me and I can identify with. Seeing my personality traits laid out so clearly will hopefully help me to better understand my strengths and my weaknesses, allowing me to focus on and improve all of those aspects.
Two distinct areas that I will absolutely need to continue working on were represented within this test. These are a common perception others have of me in that I am perceived as being insensitive and my desire to always "do everything by the book" can often put me at odds with colleagues who work differently.

It is important that I be mindful of the personalities I group with and ensure I do my best to mitigate my often blunt and focused attitude to ensure my teammates feel comfortable working with me. Regarding my desire to always follow rules, this is something I need to work on in order to trust that while my colleagues and teammates may work differently, they also have the goal of achieving the best outcome for not just themselves, but all of us.
